standup - tasks of the previous work day
day - stats for today
week - stats for the week
month - stats for the month
active - show active tasks
info - show account settings 
snooze - snooze any alerts for 30 minutes
delete - delete account
healthcheck - [threshold] detailed report with a list of tasks where the difference between active and complete is bigger than a given threshold
email - set email address
addtimeoff - add time off in hours to the current month (ex. /addtimeoff 8)
